Welcome

This blog shares ideology, policies, and updates from the Communist Party of India (Marxist). The CPI(M) fights for equality, workers' rights, and a socialist transformation of India.


Founded in 1964, CPI(M) has been at the forefront of social justice, land reforms, and the empowerment of marginalized communities. The party stands for secularism, democracy, and the upliftment of the working class, striving to build a society free from exploitation and discrimination.


Over the decades, CPI(M) has led numerous movements for land redistribution, fair wages, and the protection of civil liberties. The party has played a key role in shaping progressive policies in states like Kerala, West Bengal, and Tripura, focusing on education, healthcare, and inclusive development. Its commitment to gender equality, environmental sustainability, and communal harmony continues to inspire millions across India.


On this blog, you will find detailed articles on the party's ideology, policy initiatives, and the latest news and campaigns. We also share stories of grassroots activism, historical milestones, and the ongoing struggles for a more equitable India. Dive into interviews with party leaders, analyses of current events, and features on the global leftist movement.


We encourage readers to engage with our content, share their perspectives, and participate in discussions that shape the future of our society. Whether you are a supporter, a student, a researcher, or someone curious about leftist movements in India, this platform is your gateway to understanding the vision and work of CPI(M).


Explore our sections to learn more about our core beliefs, policies, and the latest news from the party. For more information on our ideology, policies, and news updates, please navigate through the links provided in the header. Stay connected for regular updates, event announcements, and opportunities to get involved.
